Strategy Rules:

For each symbol, identify the first 5-minute candle of each day.

Buy Entry: If price crosses above the high of the first candle, enter a long trade.

Sell Entry: If price crosses below the low of the first candle, enter a short trade.

Only one buy and one sell trade is allowed per symbol per day.

Apply the following conditions:

Target: +0.5%

Stop Loss: -0.25%

Exit Time: 15:15 (if target or SL not hit)
